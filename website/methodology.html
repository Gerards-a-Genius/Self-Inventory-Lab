Step 1: Become Self-Led

Discover Your Human Design Blueprint

Before you can change anything, you need to understand how you're uniquely designed to operate. Your Human Design is your energetic blueprint—it reveals your authentic decision-making process, how you interact with energy, and your natural way of navigating the world. This isn't just intellectual knowledge; it's about feeling the truth of your design in your body.

What You'll Discover:

  • Your unique decision-making process (Authority)
  • How you're designed to use your energy (Type & Strategy)
  • Your natural talents and gifts (Profile & Centers)
  • How to trust your body's wisdom over your mind's chatter
  • Your authentic way of being in relationships and work

The Result: You stop trying to be someone you're not and start honoring who you actually are. You move from your head to your body, creating a somatic foundation for sustainable transformation.

Energy Type

Step 2: Heal Your Parts & Patterns

Deep Healing Through Internal Family Systems

Knowing your design is powerful, but if you have protective parts—firefighters rushing in to numb pain, managers trying to control everything, or exiles holding old wounds—they'll sabotage your attempts to live authentically. IFS helps us meet these parts with compassion and heal from the inside out.

What You'll Experience:

  • Identify your protectors (managers and firefighters) keeping you stuck
  • Understand what these parts are trying to protect you from
  • Heal exiled parts carrying old wounds and shame
  • Access your authentic Self (with a capital S) that can lead with wisdom
  • Transform internal conflict into internal harmony

The Result: You stop fighting with yourself and start operating from Self-energy. The protective patterns that kept you stuck transform into allies supporting your growth. Shadow becomes integrated wisdom.

Step 3: Embody Your Authentic Path

Create Aligned, Embodied Action

Now that you know who you are and have healed what was in the way, it's time to create a life that reflects your authentic Self. This isn't about forcing or pushing—it's about embodied action that flows from your center, honoring both your Human Design and your healed inner system.

What You'll Create:

  • A clear vision aligned with your authentic self
  • Goals that honor your energy and decision-making process
  • Daily practices that support your unique needs
  • Boundaries that protect your energy and values
  • A sustainable rhythm that prevents burnout
  • Relationships that support your growth

The Result: You move through life with embodied confidence, making decisions from your body's wisdom rather than your mind's conditioning. Every action comes from an integrated, authentic place.
