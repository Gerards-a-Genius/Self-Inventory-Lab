How It Works

Your journey from inquiry to transformation

1. Initial Connection

We'll start with a brief conversation to understand what you're looking for and ensure we're a good fit. After 500+ clients, I know that the right match between your needs and my approach is essential for authentic transformation.

2. Foundation Setting

Before our first session, you'll complete a comprehensive intake form and provide your birth details for your Human Design chart. This helps me prepare for our time together and hit the ground running.

Whether it's a single session or longer program, we'll dive deep into your unique design, meet your protective parts with compassion, and facilitate somatic shifts. Every session is tailored to what's most alive for you in the moment.

4. Integration & Support

True transformation happens in the integration. You'll receive resources, somatic practices, and ongoing support to help you embody the shifts we create together. This ensures changes happen in your body, not just your mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know anything about Human Design or IFS before we work together?

Not at all! I'll teach you everything you need to know as we go. In fact, sometimes it's better to come in with fresh eyes and an open mind.

What if I'm not sure my birth time is accurate?

We'll work with what you have, and there are ways to verify your chart through our conversations. The most important thing is being open to the process.

How is this different from therapy or other coaching?

I combine the practical wisdom of Human Design with the deep healing power of IFS to address both your energetic design AND your internal parts system. This creates embodied transformation, not just intellectual understanding.

What if I don't see results right away?

Transformation is rarely linear. Some people have immediate breakthroughs, others integrate more gradually. I'm committed to supporting you through whatever timeline feels right for you.

Can I switch between services or upgrade?

Absolutely! Many clients start with a single session and then move into longer programs. We can always adjust based on what emerges.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I offer payment plans for the Intensive, 90-Day Container, and Group Pods. Transformation shouldn't be limited by finances.
